    Mass Shootings in the U.S.

    About Our Website

    Mass shootings in the United States have been at the forfront of political and news world in recent years. Throughout this website we will dive into the data from every mass shooting since 2018 in order to answer these questions: What states have the most mass shootings? Are there certain times of year in which mass shootings occur more frequently? And how many people have been affected by these events in recent years?
